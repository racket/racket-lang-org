Friday, 2:00PM EDT
Internals Nerds Unite
Hacking Racket Internals
Your chance to dig in to Racket internals and help contribute to Racket development! As the Racket community grows, there’s an increasing interest in not just making cool Racket programs, but getting into the guts of Racket itself. If that sounds interesting to you, this is your hackathon! Sam Tobin-Hochstadt will offer a brief kick-off crash course and stick around for answering questions.
